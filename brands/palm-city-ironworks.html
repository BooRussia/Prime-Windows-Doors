About Palm City Ironworks

Palm City is a Florida family shop where every scroll, leaf, and picket is drawn from raw steel by hand. No cast components. No imported finishes. The forge is on site. The artists sign their work.

Double and single entries. Arched transoms. Venetian bronze, rust patina, or blackened steel. Insulated low E glass behind the iron. Impact options for coastal homes.
